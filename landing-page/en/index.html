FAQ

The questions that avoid confusion

€49 is still an expense

It only makes sense if it saves you doubts, wasted effort and lost inquiries. The value is in getting priorities already sorted, not a long report to file away.

My website is fine as it is

It might be. The check-up exists precisely to see whether it still holds up well on mobile, contacts and clarity, or whether there are small points quietly costing you inquiries today.

Is it safe? You won't touch the site?

Yes: the check is external and non-invasive. I don't access the backend, don't try logins, don't run exploits and don't use aggressive scans.

What about privacy or GDPR?

I only flag elements visible from the outside, like banners or policy links. I don't provide legal or GDPR consulting and issue no compliance opinions.

What if you don't find anything really useful?

I won't force the report. If the check turns up too little concrete material, I'll leave you a free mini-analysis with the most useful points instead of padding out the work.
