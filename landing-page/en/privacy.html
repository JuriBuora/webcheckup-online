Privacy notice for a check-up request

Last updated: 11 July 2026.

Who handles the data

Juri Buora handles the contact data submitted through this form. For data questions or requests, email webcheckup.online@gmail.com.

What is collected

The form collects the business name, public website, sector, goal, contact details, timing and notes you choose to send. Phone/WhatsApp is optional.

Why it is used

Data is used to assess and answer the request, prepare an initial mini-analysis or, if agreed, arrange a check-up. It is not used for penetration testing, private-area access or automated decisions.

How it travels

The form uses FormSubmit to forward the request to the email address shown on this site. FormSubmit and the email provider therefore process data as external services under their own terms and privacy notices.

Retention and requests

Requests are kept only as long as needed to handle them and, if work is agreed, for any applicable administrative obligations. You can ask for access, correction or deletion by emailing the address above.

Scope

This notice covers this website's request form, not the website you submit for a check-up. Review it if the operator, providers or sales setup changes.
